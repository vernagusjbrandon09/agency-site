Our Mission & Story

Pathway Bridge Solutions LLC was founded on the belief that a person's past should not define their entire future. We saw a gap in the recruitment industry where high-potential candidates were being ignored due to automated background checks and outdated biases.

We specialize in human-centric recruiting. By providing justice-impacted individuals with professional tools and connecting them with inclusive employers, we create a bridge that benefits the candidate, the company, and society as a whole.
