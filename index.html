The Signature Mission

Why We Champion
Fair Chance Hiring

Over 70 million Americans have a criminal record. By excluding this population, companies miss out on a massive pool of loyal, high-performing talent. At Pathway Bridge, we advocate for the person, not the paper.

Higher Retention Rates

Fair chance employees show 30% higher loyalty rates, reducing long-term turnover costs for your business.

De-Risked Hiring

We utilize federal bonding programs and WOTC tax credits to ensure your hire is backed by financial security.
